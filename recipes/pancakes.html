Fluffy Chocolate Chip Pancakes

This is one of my favorite recipes to cook due to its simplicity. This recipe started as plain flat pancakes, and later I decided to experiment with the texture and flavor of the pancakes.

Ingredients and Materials

  • Your favorite pancake mix
  • Water
  • Bowl
  • Spatula
  • Pan
  • Chocolate chips (I prefer milk chocolate)
  • Stove

Time To Cook !

  1. Add water to your pancake mix until you get a consistency that is not too liquid but is still very dense to achieve a fluffy pancake.
  2. Add your desired amount of chocolate chips to the mixture.
  3. Preheat your stove for 5 minutes at a medium temperature.
  4. Pour half a cup of the mixture into your pan and add more chocolate chips for extra flavor.
  5. Watch the pancakes fluff up, and after almost a minute, flip your pancakes.
