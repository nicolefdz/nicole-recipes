Pitacos (Pizza + Tacos)

Behind the Recipe: I learned to make this fun recipe when I was little after my childhood best friend's mom made it for dinner one night. It recreates the delicious flavors of pizza in a taco shell but without all the hard work of creating an actual pizza.

Ingredients and Materials

  • Your favorite soft tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Ketchup or tomato sauce
  • Your desired toppings (For ex: corn, peperoni, jam...)
  • Pan
  • Stove or oven

Time To Cook !

  1. Preheat your pan on the stove for 1 minute, then heat your tortillas for 20 seconds.
  2. Take your tortilla from the pan and distribute the ketchup or tomato sauce.
  3. Add the shredded cheese and your desired toppings to the pitaco.
  4. Place it back on the pan until the cheese is melted while also trying not to burn the tortilla.
