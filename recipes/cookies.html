Butter Cookies

I learned to make this recipe when I was a girl scout for approximately a month and then left. This simple recipe will bring back sweet memories from your childhood and provide a fun time while you prepare the recipe.

Ingredients and Materials

  • 3 ½ cups of wheat flour
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 ¾ butter bar
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla
  • Oven
  • Cookie cutters
  • Non-stick cookie tray
  • Parchment paper
  • Toppings to decorate (optional)
  • Spatula
  • Bowl
  • Strainer
  • Measuring Cups

Time To Bake !

  1. Strain the flour.
  2. Mix the butter and sugar to form a paste, do not beat or mix too much.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla.
  4. Add the flour little by little, along with the salt in the previous mixture, until everything is perfectly unified and does not stick to your hands.
  5. Let your mix cool in the fridge for a minimum of 3 hours.
  6. Take your mix out of the fridge and use the cookie cutters to shape them while adding your preferred toppings.
  7. Place parchment paper on your cookie tray and organize your cookies.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 F (176 C) until the edges are lightly golden brown and you smell freshly baked cookies; this will take approximately 15-20 minutes.
  9. Finally, let them cool off for 10 minutes.

Bon Appétit, you made it !
